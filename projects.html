O'Talent

O'Talent is my final training project. I worked on it with 2 other developers. I contributed to the entire back-end structure. Additionally, I was involved in the front-end to finalize the project, particularly focusing on the application's responsiveness. For the back-end of this project, we used Node.js, Fastify, and Apollo Server as we designed a GraphQL API. We utilized PostgreSQL as the database management system. On the front-end, we worked with React and Tailwind CSS. The site is deployed on Netlify and is accessible. However, I am currently working on migrating the server, which was originally hosted on a virtual machine. All features of the application will soon be available.
